Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Why do you ask for my property type?

+

Because a 2-bedroom apartment takes less time than a 2-bedroom house, and we only charge you for what we clean! Different property types have different cleaning requirements and timeframes.

Do I need to be home for the services?

+

For first-time home cleanings, we ask that someone is present to let us in. After your first service, you may choose to proivde secure access or key instructions if preferred. For car wash and laundry, you do not need to be home - just provide access to your vehicle or laundry.

How does Laundry Express Service - 24 hours work?

+

Express Service guarantees pickup and return within 24 hours for Laundry. Just select the Express add-on during booking.

How does payment work?

+

When you book your service, you'll pay a deposit based on your service type (see Deposit Policy). This deposit secures your appointment time and is non-refundable. On the day of your appointment, you'll recieve an automated email and/or text with a secure link to pay the remaining balance. Full payment must be completed before service begins. Our team is unable to proceed with service until payment is confirmed.

Can I bundle services?

+

Absolutely! Bundle car wash, home cleaning, and/or laundry service to save time and money. Look for bundle offers when booking online.

What kind of detergent do you use?

+

We use non-scented detergent by default. If you'd like eco-friendly or skin-sensitive detergent, just add it to your order.

Do you bring your own water and equipment?

+

For Mobile Car Wash: Yes — our pros arrive fully equipped with water, power, and supplies. We never use your utilities.

For Home Cleaning: We bring our own vacuums and cleaning products, but we'll use your water to fill mop buckets and clean surfaces as needed.

For Laundry: Everything is done off-site using commercial-grade washers and dryers — your utilities are never used.

What if I have special instructions or needs?

+

You'll have the option to leave special notes when booking (e.g. gate codes, bag placement, pet reminders). We'll review your request before your service begins.

Is tipping required?

+

Tipping is never required, but always appreciated. 100% of tips go directly to your assigned Bubbler. You'll have the option to tip digitally after service.

How do I reschedule or cancel?

+

You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment through your confirmation link. Need to cancel? Your deposit is non-refundable, but we'll help you reschedule as needed.

Why is my deposit non-refundable?

+

We reserve your appointment time and begin scheduling your Bubbler as soon as your booking is received. Your deposit helps us cover time-sensitive preparation and holds your slot in our schedule. Because we're reserving labor and resources in advance, we're unable to refund deposits if a cancellation occurs.

You can still reschedule your appointment at no extra cost — your deposit will carry over to your new time.
