Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Do you bring your own water and equipment?

+

Yes - our pros arrive fully equipped. We bring our own water, power, and cleaning supplies. We never use your utilities unless requested.

Do I need to be home for the services?

+

For first-time home cleanings, we ask that someone is present to let us in. After your first service, you may choose to proivde secure access or key instructions if preferred. For car wash and laundry, you do not need to be home - just provide access to your vehicle or laundry.

How does same-day laundry work?

+

Book before 10 a.m. for guaranteed same-day return. After that, we'll try based on driver availability.

How does payment work?

+

When you book your service, you'll pay a deposit based on your service type (see Deposit Policy). This deposit secures your appointment time and is non-refundable. On the day of your appointment, you'll recieve an automated email and/or text with a secure link to pay the remaining balance. Full payment must be completed before service begins. Our team is unable to proceed with service until payment is confirmed.

Can I bundle services?

+

Absolutely! Bundle car wash, home cleaning, and/or laundry service to save time and money. Look for bundle offers when booking online.

What kind of detergent do you use?

+

We use non-scented detergent by default. If you'd like eco-friendly or skin-sensitive detergent, just add it to your order.
